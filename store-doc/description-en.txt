Eashy-to-use Talking Countdown Timer.


Easy-to-use Talking Countdown Timer.
For sport, exercize, game, study, etc.. Find your own use case! 

How to use.

Six preset time buttons, 10, 5, 3, 2, 1 minute(s) and 30 seconds.

It calls:
 - Every 30 seconds above 1 minute.
 - Every 5 seconds between 20 second and 1 minute.
 - Every 1 second from 20 seconds.

"5,4..." button: Pre-countdown for 5 seconds.

"Quick" button: Start immediately when push any preset button.

Flick over "Start" button and Big start button will appear. It is convenient to use by touch.
